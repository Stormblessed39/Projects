The rules for MahJong!


Open Tiles

A tile is open if there is no tile above or to the immediate left or right.

In this example the Circle 5, W, and E tiles are open because there are no tiles to their left. However, the 5 tile is not open because the Circle 5 tile is to its left.

Matchable Tiles

A number tile with another number tile of the same rank


A circle tile with another circle tile of the same rank


A bamboo tile with another bamboo tile of the same rank


Any flower tile with any other flower tile


Any season tile with any other season tile


A good rule to remember is that a tile will always match with a tile that looks exactly the same as itself
